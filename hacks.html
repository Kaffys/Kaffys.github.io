Hacks

In my free time, I love traveling to Hackathons. These are some of my projects.

iFitness

PennApps 2015 Fall

A fitness applicaton where you can create workouts on your iPhone, and send them to your Apple Watch so that you can track your workout, your heartrate, and what is next on your workout. Created using XCode 7.0 and Swift.

This was my first experience with XCode 7.0 and Swift, and I had the oppertuntity to borrow an Apple Watch for the duration of the hackathon. I recieved an Apple Developers License as well as an Apple Watch for creating a Health related application.

CPR Buddy

PennApps Winter 2014

An andriod watch application that has instructions how to do CPR. It vibrates at intervals, so the user knows when to do chest compressions.

Hemonite

Global Game Jam 2013

Hemomite was created by team effort for the 2013 Global Game Jam, in which the theme was "heartbeats". The game is a standard top down shoot 'em up experience, where keeping in line with the background heart beat it key to advancing through the game.
