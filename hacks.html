Hacks

In my free time, I love traveling to Hackathons. These are some of my projects. I travel to hackathons more as a mentor now, because I want to encourage more students to pursue programming related fields.

fishTV

Programmer | Apple TV Hackathon | November 2015

A tvOS game where you can play with your pet fish. I worked as the only programmer on a hackathon game, where the goal was to create an application for the AppleTV. I worked on a team of three other students, Shayna (Artist), IJke (Designer), Colin (Designer) to create fisTV. You can have multiple different kinds of fish, play games with your pet fish, and using the currency you get from playing games, buy new objects for your fish tank.

iFitness

PennApps 2015 Fall

A fitness application where you can create workouts on your iPhone, and send them to your Apple Watch so that you can track your workout, your heartrate, and what is next on your workout. Created using XCode 7.0 and Swift.

This was my first experience with XCode 7.0 and Swift, and I had the opportunity to borrow an Apple Watch for the duration of the hackathon. I received an Apple Developers License as well as an Apple Watch for creating a Health related application.

Hemonite

Global Game Jam 2013

Hemomite was created by team effort for the 2013 Global Game Jam, in which the theme was "heartbeats". The game is a standard top down shoot 'em up experience, where keeping in line with the background heart beat it key to advancing through the game.
